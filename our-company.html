Our Company

Established History & Missions
Aureole Information Technology Inc. (AIT) was established in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam in March 2001 as a wholly owned subsidiary of Mitani Sangyo Co., Ltd.
The company provides software development service and support with the introduction of package software in Vietnam. For software development project, BrSE (Bridge System Engineer) will be dispatched on-site to ensure smooth offshore transition. In order to develop Japanese-speaking personnel and improve service quality, the company has also incorporating training courses in Japan.
