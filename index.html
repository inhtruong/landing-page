OUR COMPANY

Established History & Missions

Aureole Information Technology Inc. (AIT) was established in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam in March 2001 as a wholly owned subsidiary of Mitani Sangyo Co., Ltd.
The company provides software development service and support with the introduction of package software in Vietnam. For software development project, BrSE (Bridge System Engineer) will be dispatched on-site to ensure smooth offshore transition.
In order to develop Japanese-speaking personnel and improve service quality, the company has also incorporating training courses in Japan.

Today, data never stops growing through the activities of people, organizations, and communication devices, and it has since become our mission to create and offer systems that can transmit a giant amount of data in an optimized fashion. AIT has so far greatly supported its customers' organizational management activities with their systems in Japan and Vietnam.
Human beings have the ability to transform. AIT wishes to continue to participate in the ever-expanding Digital Transformation.

AIT technical staffs are highly skilled technical engineers with unique knowledge and vision. Moreover, we are proud of a working environment where each individual is free to choose ways to develop and express their abilities. We welcome energetic and enthusiastic candidates who can join us in taking the company to reach for higher goals.
